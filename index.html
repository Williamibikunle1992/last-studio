Welcome to last Studio

Last Studio is the online portfolio of London based product photographer, Lucy Lang. Lucy specialises in commercial and editorial projects, and is represented in the UK by Shutter Group.

commercial shoot

Women's Collection for Brogue Brothers

Brogue Brothers commissioned Last Studio to produce an innovative editorial story to showcase the launch of their new range for women, Brogue Sisters. Located in an Edwardian town house, the brief was to build a set of images which represented their luxurious yet effortlessly cool style.

editorial shoot

the paper trail

In collaboration with Huis Magazine, Last Studio produced a set of images which highlighted the colour and clean lines of select stationary products. We aimed to build a set of images that successfully achieved this by using simple placement, bold shadow and contrasting backgrounds throughout the narrative.
